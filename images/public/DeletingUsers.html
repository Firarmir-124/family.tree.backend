Warning! After deleting your account, all your data will be lost and cannot be recovered.

  • 1. Open the app – launch it on your device.
  • 2. Go to your profile – usually, you need to tap the profile icon at the bottom corner of the screen.
  • 3. Scroll down – on the profile page, scroll all the way down.
  • 4. Find the "Delete Account" button – it should be at the very bottom of the screen.
  • 5. Tap the button – a confirmation window may appear.
